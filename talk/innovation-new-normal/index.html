Innovation on the New Normal

Abstract

Event
Talk
Location

Let’s face it: we are never going back to the way we once were before Covid-19. The world was shaken, countries have staggered, businesses have sunk, and people are gravely aggravated. When the world thought it could take the hit, it was proven wrong. The “Normal” had its loopholes, and the “New Normal” - the new buzzword in town - is making its entourage with a variety of dynamics. In this webinar we will explore on technologies that will introduce new and vast opportunities and help shape our changing needs in a changing world to be… More flexible… More capable… Less vulnerable. This webinar will be in 2 parts. Part 1 will be on May 11 which will revolve around the applications to be introduced by Artificial Intelligence, Big Data, Healthcare Technology, and Virtual/Augmented Reality… And Part 2 will be on May 18, which will revolve around the applications to be introduced by Blockchain, E-Commerce, Financial Technology, and Smart Cities.
