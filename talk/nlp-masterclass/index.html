How Computers Understand Human Language

Abstract

I had conducted an international seminar / workshop on the fundamentals of Natural Language Processing / Artificial Intelligence.The audience was mostly technical people–having MS and PhDs.

Date

5:00 PM — 6:00 PM

Seminar and Workshop

Online Masterclass on Natural Language Processing (with E-Certificates)

We invite you to apply in our “Online Masterclass on Natural Language Processing.” You may access the online application form through this link: http://tiny.cc/masterclassNLP

For this session, we will discuss how these so-called smart machines are able to “understand” the human language. Our speaker, Jude Michael Teves from the Philippines, will talk about the fundamentals of NLP, as well as share his experience working on the implementation and management of different related projects as part of ADB’s innovation team.

Meet our Speaker

Jude Michael Teves is an Artificial Intelligence Consultant at the Asian Development Bank (ADB). His current work includes implementing and managing a variety of projects in the fields of computer vision, natural language processing, data analytics, and IoT. In 2019, he earned his Master’s Degree in Data Science at the Asian Institute of Management, where he graduated as Batch Salutatorian with High Distinction, the highest academic award given by the institution.
